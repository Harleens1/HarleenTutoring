About me

Computer science graduate who does tutoring on the side. I have been tutoring kids grade 1 to 6 for more than a year. My goal is not just to teach but to inspire a love for learning, empowering students to reach their full potential. Whether it's mastering math, refining writing skills, or delving into the wonders of science, I'm here to support and motivate you every step of the way!
